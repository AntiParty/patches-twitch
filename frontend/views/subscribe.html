Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom bot account?

Simply create a new Twitch account with your desired bot name. After subscribing, you'll link it through our secure OAuth flow.

Can I change my bot account later?

Yes! You can unlink and re-link a different bot account at any time from your subscription management page.

What happens if I cancel?

You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. After that, the bot will revert to using the default account.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We never store your payment details.

Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds within 7 days of your initial subscription. Contact support for assistance.
